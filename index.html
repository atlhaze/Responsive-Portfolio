About Me

I'm an aspiring web developer. My hobbies include coding, reading, playing guitar, yoga, meditation, flow-art, drinking tea... a lot of tea, and spending time with my two dogs. I also love to sail and horsebackride in the summertime.

Various subject areas that interest me include politics, psychology, linguistics, history, culture, spirituality, philosophy, and travel. I also enjoy brushing up on the latest in astronomy, biology, animal behavioral sciences, sociology, and a load of psudo-sciences as well.

I spent the majority of time volunteering as a program director for a non-profit drug and alcohol rehabilitation center; as well as counseling individuals, leading groups and giving informative lectures on the nature of addiction.

I believe charity work and helping others is very fufilling and imperative to individual growth.
